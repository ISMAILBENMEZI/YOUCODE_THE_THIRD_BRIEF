Everything You Need to Know : Master’s Madrid

Tournament match-ups, format, schedule, and more!

The matches are set for the first global VALORANT Champions Tour event of 2025. From Novembre 14 - 24, the top 8 teams from around the world will compete in Madrid, Spain for the title of Masters Madrid Champion. Take a look below for more information on the participating teams, format, brackets and schedule.

The 2025 season began with Kickoff Tournaments across the four International Leagues - VCT Americas, VCT CN, VCT EMEA, and VCT Pacific.
